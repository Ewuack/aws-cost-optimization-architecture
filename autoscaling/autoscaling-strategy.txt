AWS Autoscaling Strategy

1. EC2 Auto Scaling
   - Scale based on CPU, memory, or request count
   - Use predictive scaling for stable workloads

2. Application Auto Scaling
   - Scale ECS tasks based on CPU/Memory
   - Scale DynamoDB based on read/write capacity

3. Scheduled Scaling
   - Scale down during off-hours
   - Reduce compute waste for non-production workloads
